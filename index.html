Pros and Cons of having Turtles as Pets

Pros

  • Small
  • Cheap to buy
  • Harmless

Cons

  • Boring for kids
  • Expensive habitat (tank and other equipment)
  • Needs fresh food
  • Turtles can carry salmonella bacteria on their shell

Conclusion

Turtles can be good pets if you're willing to risk being exposed to salmonella bacteria and have the money. Otherwise, turtles are great pets unless you're a kid who gets bored easily.
