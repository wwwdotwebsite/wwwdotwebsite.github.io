Pros and Cons of having Turtles as Pets

Pros

  • Small
  • Cheap to buy
  • Harmless

Cons

  • Boring for kids
  • Expensive habitat (tank and other equipment)
  • Needs fresh food
  • Turtles can carry salmonella bacteria on their shell

Conclusion

Turtles are not good pets, unless you have the money and are willing to take a risk of getting salmonella.
Beware turtles are boring.
